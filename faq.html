Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common IT questions. For more detailed guides, visit the Knowledge Base.

How do I reset my password?

Use the self-service password reset portal (if enabled) or contact the IT Help Desk by phone. For security reasons, we cannot send passwords via email or chat.

How do I request access to a new system?

Submit a ticket through the portal and select the request type “Access Request”. Include your manager’s name and the system you need access to.

What is the expected response time for tickets?

Response times depend on priority:

  • P1 – Critical: 15–30 minutes
  • P2 – High: 2 business hours
  • P3 – Normal: 1 business day
  • P4 – Low / Request: 2–3 business days

Can I use my personal device for work?

Personal devices may be allowed under the BYOD policy. They must comply with security standards such as disk encryption, up-to-date antivirus, and strong passwords.

How do I report a suspicious email or phishing attempt?

Do not click any links. Use the “Report Phishing” button in your email client (if available) or forward the email to security@example.com. Then open a ticket so we can investigate.
